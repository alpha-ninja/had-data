                                <a class="gray-link" href="/project/4912/log/16000#discussion-list">

                                </small>
                            <div style="position:relative">
                                <div id="post-body-16000"><p>I will also be updating these boxes this year to be mostly enclosed with plastic, with a red mulch and a red backer.  There will be an artificial muscle lid that will automatically open and close when the box heats up or cools down.  This should dramatically increase yeild.  I always laugh at the mars one hipsters who think growing food on mars will be easy when we haven't even effectively terraformed earth.  There is a specific challenge to growing food at very high altitudes, one being solar radiation, and the other being loss of heat.  I will attempt to solve these problems with my box system.  </p></div>
