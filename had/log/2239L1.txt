                            <h2><a href="/project/2239/log/7081-prefab-shielding">Prefab shielding</a></h2>
                            <small class="authorship">a year ago  &bull;
                                <a class="gray-link" href="/project/2239/log/7081#discussion-list">
                                    0 comments
                                </a>
                                </small>
                            <div style="position:relative">
                                <div id="post-body-7081"><p>Hi, we have been comparing footprints and placement.&nbsp; At this point some of the smaller prefab almost fit, but not quite.&nbsp; The big issue is the 2.54mm sized castellations.&nbsp; They are too close for even the smallest shields.&nbsp; Between that and antenna placement.&nbsp; We may need to adjust the overall board layout.&nbsp; Almost all common nRF51822 modules have shields, but they also have really poor antenna placement (according to manufacturer recommendations).&nbsp; For now, we will not include component shielding.</p></div>
                            </div>
                        </li>
                    
                        <li>
                            <h2><a href="/project/2239/log/11735-design-updates">Design updates</a></h2>
                            <small class="authorship">8 months ago  &bull;
                                <a class="gray-link" href="/project/2239/log/11735#discussion-list">
                                    0 comments
                                </a>
                                </small>
                            <div style="position:relative">
                                <div id="post-body-11735"><p>As with all the other Flex Modules, I will be improving the castellation design shortly.  In addition, I may try to add more GPIO pins.  As the shield and the 3dB Molex antenna are similar heights, I can get away with closer trace routes with that antenna.  So in addition to it being probably the highest gain chip antenna (especially for the price), it also improves the design.  I'll also likely move to a 4 layer stack up, so I need to recalculate the co-planar waveguide.  We should be able to start selling these when ready in the next month or two as a Flex Module is a "subassembly" not requiring certification.  We will try to get it certified when it becomes popular.</p></div>
                            </div>
                        </li>

So I know what the pin outs will look like (which held this board back).  So I should be sending a new design with the 3dB antenna and shield to OSHPark this weekend.  The design just fits the Tag Connect pogo programmer and antenna just outside the shield.  I also added optional components - a reset button, an RGB LED (in shield), and a 32k crystal.<br></p></div>
