                            <div style="position:relative">
                                <div id="post-body-22171"><p>Found that the Jakks Eyeclops don&apos;t make good othoscopes. They tend to be very good remote microscopes for looking at patients skin close up. Can&apos;t view inside someone&apos;s ear, mouth, nose. or eye as the orifice is too deep for the gadget. Has to be right up to the surface. Also the light is blinding to the eye but you could look at someone&apos;s cornea with it. I&apos;m going to stick with the Harbor Frieght Inspection Camera snake camera. It will insert further into the patient&apos;s orifice for viewing.<br><br>JAKKS EYECLOPS</p><p><img style="width: 270px; height: 143px;" src="https://cdn.hackaday.io/images/327731438796071766.jpg" height="143" width="270">HARBOR FREIGHT SNAKE CAM<br></p><p><img style="width: 260px; height: 261px;" src="https://cdn.hackaday.io/images/3416691438796255379.jpg" height="261" width="260"></p></div>
