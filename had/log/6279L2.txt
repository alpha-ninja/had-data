                                <div id="post-body-19982"><p><img src="https://cdn.hackaday.io/images/6428251435270770985.png"></p><p>I want to propose you the idea of a separate programmer for the Freescale CPU, I designed the above block following the schematic of Teensy 3.1 at <a target="_blank" href="http://www.pjrc.com/teensy/schematic.html">the pjrc.com website</a>.
