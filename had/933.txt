                    <h5>Description</h5>
                    <div class="description">We are using a galaxy HM3300 BT headset with NFC as donor device. Re-casing I guess you would call it. We are actually working on a real one from scratch, but thought this would be a fun contest to get in, and see how people felt about getting hold of the real deal. ;-)</div>

                <div class="section section-details">

                            Details
                        
                    </h5>
                    

                    
                        <div class="post-content details-content">
                            <p><p name="f4f7">So I don't know about you, but my son, friends and myself have wanted a Star Trek communicator since the first time I/we saw Riker touch it and talk to the enterprise. This is the predecessor to an currently developing project. More or less it was our proof of concept. ;)</p><p name="0bad">We are re-purposing a current bluetooth headset with NFC to look and function like the communicator that all of us fell in love with.&nbsp;</p><p name="ec76">The project is close to complete, and nobody read the rules until a few minutes ago. We have kept some form of logs, usually some pics of what we were working on, but we'll fill in the blanks for everyone. </p>
