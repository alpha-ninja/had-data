                <div class="section section-details">
                    <h5>
                        
                            Details
                        
                    </h5>
                    

                    
                        <div class="post-content details-content">
                            <p><p>The 12axis <a href="http://hackaday.io/project/2236-flex-modules">Flex Module</a> contains an LSM9DS1TR and BME280.  The LSM9DS1TR is an accelerometer, gyroscope, and magnetometer or as commonly referred to as a 9-axis sensor.  The BME280 provides barometric pressure, humidity, and temperature sensing.  So I am calling it a 12axis Flex Module.<br></p><p>The board is configured for i2c communication.  LSM9DS1TR has a separate address for the magnetometer from the accelerometer and gyro.  The address least significant bit is selectable for those as well as the BME280.  I have routed the first interrupt of the accelerometer/gyro as INTG and the magnetometer as INTM.  The data enable pin of the accelerometer/gyro and the data ready pin of the magnetometer are also exposed.</p><p>Defaults addresses for the LSM9DS1 are as in datasheet and not common to most drivers based on the LSM9DS0.  So adjust any examples from Adafruit, Sparkfun, etc. accordingly.</p><p>LSM9DS1:<br></p><ul><li>Accelerometer/Gyro 0x6A  (0x6B A.G solder bridge shorted)</li><li>Magneto 0x1C (0x1D A.M solder bridge shorted)</li></ul><p><br>BME280<br></p><ul><li>0x76 (0x77 A.B solder bridge shorted)</li></ul>
                                
                            </p>
                        </div>
