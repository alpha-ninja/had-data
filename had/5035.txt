                <div class="section section-description">
                    <h5>Description</h5>
                    <div class="description">The butlerBOT it's a helping robot for the ones that can't carry things by themselves. Integrating the power of an arduino an their modules (RFID and ultrasonic) the buttlerBOT can make things like: <br /><br /># Deliver things that are on its plate to where the RFID emitter is. (RFID emitter could be on a stick or in a bracelet)<br /># Avoid obstacles<br /># Used as a table <br /><br />Programming the arduino on board with a buzzer, and some leds it could remind to take pills during the day, and with a small compartment it could storage the pills. <br /><br />Adding a HELP Button in the robot could be useful too in case of an emergency. Also in the stick or bracelet it will be one, and if it is touched several times the alarm will be activated, sending a SMS to the person in charge. <br /><br />Integrating a radio and a torch could make this Butler more useful and entertaining.</div>
                </div>

                            Details
                        
                    </h5>
                    

                    
                        <div class="post-content details-content">
                            <p><h2>Inspiration</h2><p>Many elders does not have enough strength to lift up certain things and move it to other places. Others need an extra hand because one is occupied by a stick. So the Butler BOT will help those who need it, providing transport for drinks, food, pills, magazines, and so on. Also provides entertainment, a torch and a SOS button.</p><p>Future improvements, add a brush to clean the house while it moves and program a cleaning function. Because some people does not have the will to clean the house.<br></p><hr><h2>Functionality</h2><p>The functionality of this robot it's very wide, but the first idea is to make it work as an autonomous robot that will reach the owner by only activating one switch (RFID emitter). The other functions add some atractive to make it part of the day a day for the elders.</p>
                                
                            </p>
