                    <h5>Description</h5>
                    <div class="description">TinyLEDs are watch battery based and are programmable through a Tag Connect SWD and large pads under the design.  The board comes with an on/off switch and sets of serial LEDs that are in fashionable patterns and shapes.</div>
                </div>
            

            
                <div class="section section-details">
                    <h5>
                        
                            Details
                        
                    </h5>
                    

                    
                        <div class="post-content details-content">
                            <p><p>Designed to experiment with smaller lighting concepts.  We will be trying to mix in fashion and ease of use to make electronics accessible.  TinyLEDs are designed to be worn or mount on any apparel or be part of an accesory.</p>
                                
                            </p>
                        </div>
