                            <h2><a href="/project/6846/log/23581-general-description-of-top-navette">General Description Of Top Navette</a></h2>
                            <small class="authorship">8 hours ago  &bull;
                            <a class="gray-link" href="/project/6846/log/23581#discussion-list">
                                0 comments
                            </a>
                            </small>
                            <p><strong></strong><p><img src="https://cdn.hackaday.io/images/9866821439841500947.png"></p><p><strong>Why we  use Top Navette?</strong></p><p><strong></strong>When you want to know the most efficient road from your Home to your JOB without needing to buy a expensive product that it have more features useless in your case. Then, Top Navette is the best solution. It can give your the best roads and it will guide you  <span class="redactor-invisible-space"><span class="redactor-invisible-space"></span></span><span class="redactor-invisible-space"><span class="redactor-invisible-space"></span></span><strong><span class="redactor-invisible-space"><span class="redactor-invisible-space"></span></span></strong>by names of cities that you Now.<br><strong></strong></p><p>Top Navette store all the regular shuttle in SD card and when the user need to know what is the best road to his regular shuttle, Top Navette will generate from his personal database what is the best roads for a specific time.</p><p><strong>The features of  Top Navette </strong></p><p><span class="redactor-invisible-space"></span>Top Navette is an <span class="short_text"><span class="hps">autonomy</span></span> system but  for more performance and options you can connect your Smart Phone or your PC to it. Every time, it authenticate with your device it will synchronize with a specific application of Top Navette installed in the device.</p><p>So here, your personal data is saved in your device when you want to go in another shuttle with another car have Top Navette. All you need to do just connect your device to Top Navette and it will update the data to it.</p><p>When the communities of Top Navette become much bigger by using it in many cars, you would like to update daily your Top Navette  to become more efficient. This is done by allow the application to synchronize with the server (VAN) and then your device will send the last update to your Top Navette when they connecting by Bluetooth.</p><p> If your device is a Smart Phone and you have an access to Internet by 3G, you can get the most perfect roads by synchronizing with the server and get the instant data about road traffic.</p><p>You can use Top Navette or the application in your device to schedule your daily planning and get in every shuttle the ideal time and the best road.</p><p><strong>How we keep your shuttle privacy ?</strong></p><p><strong></strong>So this is almost all the data structure in Top Navette and Smart Phone</p><p> <img src="https://cdn.hackaday.io/images/883631439842992730.png"></p><p>So when your phone synchronize with the server, it will send only &quot;Street&quot; &amp; &quot;City&quot;. The &quot;UserStreet&quot; and &quot;UserCity&quot;  describe how you using &quot;Street&quot; and &quot;City&quot;, so they will stay in Top Navette or in your device application without sending them to the server.</p><p>The server all it need is the weight of the street.</p><p><strong>What is weight of the street ?</strong></p><p><span class="redactor-invisible-space">It&apos;s an argument that helps us to calculate the <span class="short_text"><span class="hps">expected time</span> to reach your destination and this argument is exit into &quot;Street&quot;.</span></span></p><p><span class="redactor-invisible-space"><span class="redactor-invisible-space">So the server collect all the weight of the same street from all the user and calculate the average weight in interval of time (like from 7.30 to 7.45) <br><br></span></span></p>
                                
                            </p>
