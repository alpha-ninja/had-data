                <div class="section section-details">
                    <h5>
                        
                            Details
                        
                    </h5>
                    

                    
                        <div class="post-content details-content">
                            <p><p>This board is part of Fyber Labs <a href="http://hackaday.io/project/2236-Flex-Modules">Flex Module</a> project.</p><p>We are utilizing a generally available JST connector that supports multiple amps.&nbsp; We chose the 4 pin version as it will easily accommodate common 3 lead 5V RGB LED strips as well as the 4 pin versions.&nbsp; With some 4 pin strips this adds the ability to split and splice the strips in multiple directions using simple connectors.&nbsp; We will be discussing this further in the <a href="http://hackaday.io/project/3083-Dance-Kit" target="_blank">Dance Kit </a>demo project.</p>
                                
                            </p>
