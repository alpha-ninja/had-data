                <div class="section section-description">
                    <h5>Description</h5>
                    <div class="description">I have an Audi A4 from '96 and it hasn't got a onboard computer so I'm developping one for it. <br />It features a Stellaris launchpad with the LM4F120 Cortex M4 microcontroller (not the latest TIVA version), a 2.4" TFT with the ILI9341 controller, temperature sensors for interior and exterior temperatures and later on bluetooth connection, alarm integration and OBD diagnostic readings. <br /></div>
                </div>
            

            
                <div class="section section-details">
                    <h5>
                        
                            Details
                        
                    </h5>
                    

                    
                        <div class="post-content details-content">
                            <p><p>The Stellaris board will be the responsible for controlling the display, read the temperatures from the internal and the external sensors, reading the cluster alarms (via I/O expander) like fuel warning, coolant temperature, oil level and brake problems, and user interface buttons.</p><p>Latter it will be possible to read OBD diagnostics either by direct UART connection or via Bluetooth to the OBD-Bluetooth adapter.</p>
                                
                            </p>
                        </div>
