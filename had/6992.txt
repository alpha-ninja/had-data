                <div class="section section-description">
                    <h5>Description</h5>
                    <div class="description">Instead of making a cool watch you will throw away in a few years that has no aesthetic or heirloom value.  Wear your favorite watch while making it smart.  The smart sleeve wraps around the band (might need to increase the band length).  Will provide haptic notifications and simple capacitive input as well as fitness tracking.</div>
                </div>

                    <h5>
                        
                            Details
                        
                    </h5>
                    

                    
                        <div class="post-content details-content">

Flex board wrapped around bracelet width curve and silicone&apos;d for protection.</p>
                                
                            </p>
                        </div>
