The differentiating factor between The Smart Garden Mini and other automated hydroponic food growing systems is that the Root Zone is climate controlled. In addition, The Smart Garden Mini maximizes the economics of food growth by 1) Using the sun instead of light bulbs, 2) Optimizing water usage, and 3) minimizing energy usage.
