                <div class="section section-details">
                    <h5>
                        
                            Details
                        
                    </h5>
                    

                    
                        <div class="post-content details-content">
                            <p><p>1. Take a cigarette lighter with LED</p><p><a href="http://i45.tinypic.com/24lvvx2.jpg">http://i45.tinypic.com/24lvvx2.jpg</a></p><p>2. Take it apart</p><p><a href="http://i47.tinypic.com/16h2mv5.jpg&lt;br">http://i47.tinypic.com/16h2mv5.jpg</a></p><p>3. Take an ATMEL ATtiny13, DIP8 capsule, and squeeze it into the batteries holder. Also, replace the original white LED from the lighter with an IR (Infra Red) LED.</p><p><a href="http://i49.tinypic.com/20p940n.jpg&lt;br">http://i49.tinypic.com/20p940n.jpg<br></a></p><p>4. Wire everything as in the schematic diagram. The LED does not need a series resistor. Internal resistance of the cell batteries are doing a pretty good job in limiting the LED current.</p><p><a href="http://i46.tinypic.com/2i1guwz.jpg&lt;br">http://i46.tinypic.com/2i1guwz.jpg</a></p><p>5. Program your microcontroller with the following BASCOM code:</p><pre>'''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''<br>'<br>' Infrared Remote Control Transmitter for Nikon D90<br>' -------------------------------------------------<br>'<br>'  v0.2<br>'  Written by RoGeorge<br>'  2010.01.03<br>'  Compiler BASCOM-AVR v1.11.9.1<br>'<br>' Description:<br>' DIY replacement for Nikon ML-L3 Infra Red (IR) Remote Control<br>'<br>' After power up the microcontroller send a<br>'  "TAKE PICTURE" IR command to a Nikon camera<br>'  then enter in power down mode.<br>'<br>' It should work with:<br>'  Nikon N65, N75, Coolpix 8400, 8800, Pronea S, Nuvis S &amp; Lite Touch Zoom,<br>'  Nikon D40, D40x, D50, D60, D70, D80 &amp; D90 Digital SLR Cameras<br>'<br>' It works (tested) with:<br>'  - Nikon D90<br>'<br>' Schematic:<br>'  - 1 x ATtiny13<br>'  - 1 x normal open micro switch between batteries (push button)<br>'  - 3 x 1.5V SR41 (AG3) button cells as power source<br>'  - 1 x IR LED between OC0B (PIN 6 = LED anode) and GND (PIN 4 = LED cathode)<br>'  - NO curent limiter resistor is necessary for the IR LED<br>'<br>' IR LED waveform out (100% modulated with 38.4 KHz carrier):<br>'  -  2000 us on<br>'  - 27830 us off<br>'  -  390 us on<br>'  -  1580 us off<br>'  -
