                    <h5>Description</h5>
                    <div class="description">My printable robot arm is inspired by the well known industry robots, but printable.<br />The goal is to develop a open source robot arm to use in private or small businesses and make robot development available for every one.  <br />The arm should lift about 2 kg enough to perform every day tasks.  Currently robot arms are expensive or small and weak, or clumpy. <br />Industrial robots are expensive and dangerous and for that not suitable for using at home or schools. <br />A Open Source printable robot can build,used and developed by every one. <br />Robots are still expensive and hard to operate, but this must not longer be the case. <br />A cheap 3d printed robot arm could be used by disabled persons to manage their daily needs better and more independent. Operate at dangerous but not so developed places. <br />Remote controlled you could even us the robot to open your door if you forgot your keys.<br /><br />The future Arm will include a moving base. <br /><a href='http://chaozlabs.blogspot.de/2015/01/3d-printable-robot-arm.html' target="_blank">http://chaozlabs.blogspot.de/2015/01/3d-printable-robot-arm.html</a></div>
