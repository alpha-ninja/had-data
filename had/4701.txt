                    <h5>Description</h5>
                    <div class="description">The aim of this project is to alert Emergency Services of a traffic accident as quickly as possible with accurate information. This would help Emergency Services locate a traffic accident as fast a possible.<br /><br />The product consists of a small device, which is placed in the vehicle. The device contains a GSM module, GPS module and an accelerometer. Using the accelerometer, the device detects collisions and estimates it's severity. If a certain threshold is reached, communication with predefined numbers is initiated (through SMS). Data, such as Geo-position and severity of the impact are transmitted via SMS.<br /><br />The product is aimed at the general public so cost effectiveness is a priority.</div>
                </div>
            

            
                <div class="section section-details">
                    <h5>
                        
                            Details
                        
                    </h5>
                    

                    
                        <div class="post-content details-content">
                            <p><p><strong>The Problem</strong></p><p><br>Accidents happen all the time and around the globe. <br>Emergency services receive calls all the time of accidents (mostly not fatal), and rush to the scene.<br>The least we want is to delay the call or worse failing to give accurate information of the accident.<br>Some accidents are not discovered immediately if they happen in rural areas (40% of all accidents are rural), some are discovered minutes and hours after the accident.<br>In most serious accidents, 5 minutes could be a life or death situation.</p><p><br><strong>Proposed Solution</strong></p><p><br>In view of this fact, it is necessary that through technology we would reduce delays cause by human error, to a minimum.<br>The proposed solution requires no human intervention and will alert emergency services instantly following a crash.<br>Installation of the device is very easy. It only requires a 12v DC input from the Car and the case should be attached firmly using strong double sided tape, (I am currently proposing the underside of the driver's seat but this will be open for research and discussion). The case will be insulated with high impact foam from the inside to protect the components upon impact. <br>A battery will ensure that supply will not be disrupted in case of car power failure.<br>The device will keep track of the <strong>g-force</strong> felt by the car upon impact, and the GPS position. 2 accelerometers will be at work and their average is considered the true value. However if the difference between them is greater that 5g the largest value wins, hence reducing the possibility of a failure.<br>If the result is &gt; 15g (this still to be determined and researched) the GSM module sends an SMS to multiple numbers (including the Emergency Service).</p><p>An SOS button will also be included in cases of other emergencies which do not involve crashes.</p>
                                
                            </p>
                        </div>
