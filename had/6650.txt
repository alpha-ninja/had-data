                <div class="section section-description">
                    <h5>Description</h5>
                    <div class="description">POISED captures images using one laser, and in real time another laser<br />renders those images; it is a laser scanner - a device which precisely and<br />immediately extracts and reflects two separate laser beams in order to<br />create two distinct raster scans. One of these is an input scan. It<br />generates a pattern of light projected in front of it. This raster scan<br />area contains an attached circuit which measures the amount of reflected<br />light off subjects within the scan area and directs it to the second laser.<br />This second laser generates a second raster scan, which creates an image<br />based on the information collected from the circuit. In the final version<br />of this project, I will create five of these devices and engineer a<br />rotating platform to enable panoramic image capture and display. I will<br />upgrade these devices with high wattage laser diodes of various wavelengths<br />and POISED will display multiple-colored and architectural scale unique<br />laser projections of moving images. </div>
                </div>
