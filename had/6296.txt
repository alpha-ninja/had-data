                    <h5>Description</h5>
                    <div class="description">I'm an open water swimmer and I'm often out training with many other swimmers and we always discuss the water temperature - especially during winter.<br /><br />Other parts of New Zealand have scientific grade public temperature monitoring available. New Zealand's biggest city, Auckland, doesn't. So I set out to build a sensor that would provide good enough accuracy and online monitoring.<br /><br />Basic requirements:<br /> - battery powered, at least 2 weeks life<br /> - cheap enough that if it goes missing I don't care<br /> - rugged enough to survive a good storm<br /> - reproducible, other beaches in NZ could do with this too<br /> - can be used as a template for others to wanting a battery powered environmental sensor</div>
