        <div class="container post-content">
            <p>Secure
#IoT Idea #IAMCIty 
</p><p>*
For &#x201C;Intelligent Autonomous system for Monitoring and Control of
Intersection&#x201D;</p><p>*
Brought to life through #2015HackadayPrize contest.</p><p>*
Ultimate goal of this Hackaday Project as such will be #IAMCIty Proof
of Concept (PoC).</p><p>[
Concept of operation &#x2013; Final version 06.08.2015 ]</p><p>Introduction
(only for information):</p><p>-
Numbering is in order of building-up the foundations. Differently
put, it can be seen as numbering every piece of puzzle when put in
place. Thus after all pieces are within the puzzle frame and in the
right order, the whole Image of puzzle can be seen. And only then the
puzzle&apos;s Image can make sense.</p><p>-
Concept is presented from 1st person to give actual sense
of the base that will support as foundation the following
actual implementation.</p><p>-
With all that introduction in mind, this is the fundamental part of
IAMCIty, for 1st technical details, please check this
presentation  from 06.08 project update - <a href="https://drive.google.com/file/d/0B6S44jnEBxzyTFFLYmN3Z1FVWUk/view">https://drive.google.com/file/d/0B6S44jnEBxzyTFFLYmN3Z1FVWUk/view</a> .</p><p>- Intersection, a simple definition taken from acting laws: That is the area created by two or more roadways at their junction, 
which can be at any angle.<br></p><br><p>
IAMCity = ) 
</p><p>1.
Monitoring &#x2013; This is the core functionality I have. To process
Imaging data and create meta-data, upon which the Decision making
process takes place. In that sense the data from an Imaging sensor
will be gathered only for the needs of my Digital Signal Processing
to result in meta-data. Once done the sensor data will be discard. To
state it clearly - <strong>NO</strong> Imaging data what so ever will be kept
of the passing traffic on my intersection, pedestrians or drivers of
vehicles. To have that data is not required, nor can be of better
value, for the actual Decisions-making progress once there&apos;s sensible
meta-data. Last, but not least an important notion is that: Based on
the meta-data the reverse process of creating the source Image is
considered to be close to impossible, if not impossible.</p><p><a name="__DdeLink__301_1017997111"></a>
2. Intelligent &#x2013; In relation
to my decision making process I&apos;m not an A.I. in any way. But have
the flexibility, to an extend that, I make my own decisions and they
can respond to an evolving environment. A single decision of mine
depend both on pre-defined rules and gathered meta-data. Because the
gather meta-data can result in new, additional rules, i.e. generated
another set of rules which is added to the pre-defined one. Then
there&apos;s the directive that: No generated rule can overrule a
pre-defined one.</p><p>3.
Autonomous &#x2013; I&apos;m operating on my own. I do NOT need a central
managing or control system. Also my goal is NOT to give &apos;remote
access&apos; to my input or output devices, including built-in and as well
outside peripherals. A good example is that through my systems no one
will be able to receive control to the end-points I&apos;m connected to
(as actuators for example). Simply because no such functionality
exists within my programming. Yet the following case is considered
and taken care of: There can be a situation where I&apos;m not capable to
handle the end-points (again as actuators for example). Therefore I
can decide to release them from my control so other system can take
control <strong>ONLY </strong>over the
end-points, not me. As I&apos;m autonomous. For control to be given back
to me all systems must go through reset and an initialization
process, including the actual boot-up of my core system, will take
place.</p><p>4.
Control &#x2013; Only when no information signaling is capable of
resolving an immanent situation, then I will be able to take control
over &#x201C;Traffic Signaling&#x201D;. This action will be completely
AUTONOMOUS, i.e. not executed by a remote command or governor.
Because that simply does not exist within my programming.</p><p>For
quick example:</p><p>-
I can use the LED Traffic Lights to close an intersection where a
traffic accident has taken place. NOT prior of that !</p><p>-
Change the LENS of LED Traffic Lights for better perceptions from the
participants in the traffic. Of course that will be taken upon
determining <strong>and</strong> evaluating a changed environmental conditions
. And of course if such technically solutions is available on the
intersection I&apos;m installed, locally.</p><p>5.
Modes of operations &#x2013; Basic, Advanced, Fail-safe, Stand-by/HOLD</p><p>5.1.
Basic</p><p>-
Monitoring</p><p>-
Information signaling only</p><p>-
Statistical data acquisition during operation in this mode.</p><p>Please
refer to (3) for more information about my respect toward Personal
Privacy.</p><p>5.2.
Advanced</p><p><a name="__DdeLink__315_1017997111"></a>
- Includes and continues every
operation already undergoing from Basic Mode</p><p>-
Traffic signaling</p><p>Please
refer to (4) for more information about only when I&apos;ll be using
Traffic signaling</p><p>5.3.
Fail-safe (Emergency mode &#x2013; in regard to the Intersection, NOT me
or my systems):</p><p>-
Includes and continues every operation already undergoing from
Advanced mode</p><p><a name="__DdeLink__320_1017997111"></a>
- Starting to provide
meta-data information in human-understandable way. Only as upstream
communication and only to a pre-defined communication end-point at
the outside world THAT I can authenticate. Other way to state that
would be: Only one-directional communication channel from me to the
outside world is going to be opened. And only when no decision chain
can achieve a final outcome &#x2013; a decision about the current
situation. Which is to conclude that there&apos;s some extra-ordinary
situation taking place on my intersection; to which I am not capable
to handle on my own. Privacy will be respected without any &#x201C;bending&#x201D;
of the term. Actual 1:1 Image/Video streaming are not considered
here, only meta-data will be streamed.</p><p>5.4.
Stand-by/HOLD</p><p>NB:
Does not mean a Power stand-by is executed !</p><p>-
No monitoring</p><p>-
No control</p><p>-
No statistical data</p><p>-
No meta-data streaming</p><p>That
only means every operation of mine will be on HOLD.</p><p>NB:
<strong>Security</strong> has number 0 ! Not 6. 
</p><p>0.
Security - As autonomous system I must guaranty my own security. And
also of any external component that I&apos;m to access prior to the actual
use. That is a requirement for me to be able to uptake the care and
any action toward guarantying the safety of my intersection. In that
manner there will be 3 main focus.</p><p><strong>Only</strong>
2 are into the focus of current PoC.</p><p>0.1
Activity</p><p>-
Every activity from internal operations to outside actions I perform
will be logged. And kept record for at least 1 year = 365 days as
thumb rule. This is my 1st line of defense against me as a
system being compromised.</p><p><a name="__DdeLink__317_1017997111"></a>
- Including the decision
process preceding them.</p><p>-
If a predefined number of count is reached I&apos;ll send notification to
a pre-defined communication end-point at the outside world THAT I can
authenticate. Examples would be Authorized Personal or Agency, but
still that would be only upstreaming communication. The notification
hold information about the fact that I&apos;m taking too many actions to
regulate and/or guaranty the safety on my intersection. My 3 modes of
operations will continue, but the notification will be made visible
<strong>and</strong> locally on my intersection after the first 24 hours. To
signalize the traffic for the possibility of high risk to lower
safety present on the intersection. The end-point producing the actual signal will be do so in a way that it does NOT affect the traffic. Yet
visible to the participants in the traffic so they can safe-guard
themselves and also notify the Authorized side by good will ;-)</p><p>This
event can mean 1 of 2 things:</p><p>a.
This is a troublesome intersection</p><p>b.
I&apos;m operating above my Actions count limitation (which can vary per
intersection)</p><p>0.2.
Communication - I&apos;ll not give control over my system, actuators or
analytics features. Yet I will be accepting one and only one command
&#x2013; Mode of Operation. Thus I can be switched between modes, but the
execution of that mode still will be autonomous, including the
escalating or not of my Mode of Operation. My communication will be
for 2 main purposes:</p><p>0.2.1.
Remote set of the &#x201C;Mode of Operation&#x201D;</p><p>Quick
example: I can be set in Advanced Mode of operation, but that would
be the only &amp; solely remote command accept. From that point on
any Mode switching to Fail-safe or Basic Mode I will do
interdependently.</p><p>0.2.2.
Upstream valuable (but only) meta-data for guarantying Intersection
safety &#x2013; Can be performed only based on my decision.</p><p>0.3
Physical security from unauthorized access &#x2013; not into the scope of
current PoC.</p><p>===============
END OF CONCEPT OF OPERATIONS ===============</p><p> Good Luck to All and especially to All Fellow HACKADOERS :-)</p><p>Dimitar Tomov<br></p>
