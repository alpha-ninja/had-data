                        <div class="post-content details-content">
                            <p><p>The ISDB-T Receiver is a project that implement a fully tunable ISDB-T Receiver on the BeagleBone Black.</p><span class="mw-headline">Feature</span><ol><li>ISDB-T transmits a HDTV channel and a mobile TV channel <strong>1seg</strong> within one channel.</li><li>ISDB-T can be received indoors with a simple indoor antenna.</li><li>Implementation of open reception instead of CAS protection present in ISDB-T standard. Ensuring open reception is very critical in third world countries as entertainment should be available to the common man in a <span class="highlight begin selected">fr<span class="highlight end selected">ee</span> manner.<br></span></li></ol><p>The lastest version of the ISDB-T Receiver improves upon the previous version by adding a new GUI, with Electronic program guide(EPG) <br></p><p>I faced some challenges during the creation of the digital TV receiver box. The main challenge of this project was choosing the proper Linux Kernel. <span class="st">it was really hard for me to <span class="st"> develop Linux device drivers for USB ISBD-T TV stick.</span></span></p><p><strong>My project kickoff </strong></p><iframe style="width: 500px; height: 281px;" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/UtK4nWkgGs4" allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="281" width="500"></iframe>Implementing the GUI<iframe style="width: 500px; height: 281px;" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/qaNFHqjHmEg" allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="281" width="500"></iframe>
