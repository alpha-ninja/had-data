                    <h5>Description</h5>
                    <div class="description">The Health Mate bracelet is a device that allows hospitals and/or individuals to track their pacients/self status in real time remotely for a better understanding of what the underlying body metrics (heart rate, temperature, humidity, oxygen levels) mean, and analyze the data for diagnosing or prioritizing pacients in real time when needed (e.g. a crowded ER room with people wearing the Health Mate provide enough data to observe the individual and act immediatly on the pacient that needs immediate care), and also provide long-term observations in changes of bodily functions that could point out severe disorders occuring even before they become life-threatening by analyzing data using machine learning and providing notifications to the individual wearing it.<br />- Hospital use ( ER rooms and non-invasive patient monitoring )<br />- Personal use ( Smartphone app for instant detection of changes of vital conditions )<br />We want to make the world better by keeping our fellow humans alive !</div>

                            <p><p>First connection of the Health Mate bracelet to the programming interface using ESPlorer and NodeMCU flasher, using the API to check the values of the fotodiode used for pulse detection.</p><p>We are really glad to see that the circuitry worked on first try and programming this platform is as easy as connecting the FTDI -&gt; USB converter to the PC and running 2 programs ( the ESP 8266 flasher , and the ESPlorer ).
