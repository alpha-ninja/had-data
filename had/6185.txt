                <div class="section section-description">
                    <h5>Description</h5>
                    <div class="description">This project is about building a compact and portable driver for laptop LCD screens. Image data will be received over HDMI, power for the logic and backlighting using USB. The converted data is transmitted according to the VESA standard over LVDS.</div>
                </div>
            

            
                <div class="section section-details">
                    <h5>
                        
                            Details
                        
                    </h5>
                    

                    
                        <div class="post-content details-content">
                            <p><p>The design is based on the design from TI: <a href="http://www.ti.com/lit/an/slla325a/slla325a.pdf" target="_blank">HDMI to LVDS</a>. For full HD support dual channel LVDS is needed. This forced the choice for the combination of the TFP401A with the DS90C387A.</p><p>To power the logic a 5v - 3v3 buck-convertor design was used based on the TPS62082 simple switcher. For the backlight the TI LMR62014 simple switcher was chosen to step up the voltage to 20V.</p>
                                
                            </p>
                        </div>
