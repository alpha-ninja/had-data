                <div class="section section-description">
                    <h5>Description</h5>
                    <div class="description">This board is designed to buffer to ADC and perform a 2nd order low pass filter for Spectra Symbol flex sensors.  These sensors can be glued directly to flexible PCB.</div>
                </div>
            

            
                <div class="section section-details">
                    <h5>
                        
                            Details
                        
                    </h5>
                    

                    
                        <div class="post-content details-content">
                            <p><p>This board is part of Fyber Labs <a href="http://hackaday.io/project/2236-Flex-Modules" target="_blank">Flex Module</a> project.</p><p>Flex sensor input to ADC will be essentially DC voltage so filtering isn't critical.&nbsp; The buffer provides uniform impedance and somewhat linear use of the 0-3.3V ADC input for the resistance range of the sensor.</p><p>Sample 4.5" and 2.2" flex sensors required significant break-in.  Resistance varies depending on amount of break-in and will likely increase with age/use until failure.<br><br>2.2" sample flex break in (2 minutes of ~1/4" circumference bend hand rolled through flex sensor).</p><table><thead><tr><td></td><td>Sample1</td><td>Sample2</td><td>Sample3</td><td>Sample4</td></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Initial</td><td>33-33-45</td><td>20-20-39</td><td>29-29-34.5</td><td>31-31-44</td></tr><tr><td>During</td><td>28-35-60</td><td>31-36-77</td><td>31-42-79</td><td>32-75-115</td></tr><tr><td>After<br></td><td>40-120-180</td><td>40-118-150</td><td>44-88-140</td><td>39-88-150</td></tr></tbody></table><p>Resistance in KOhm  (reverse bend, relaxed, forward bend)</p><p>So 2.2" range is around 20-150KOhm.&nbsp; 4.5" is 8-90KOhm.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p><p>LTSpice simulations were stepped through a sensor resistance range of 8K-180KOhm showing the response for these channels utilizing a ST TSX561 op-amp and 150KOhm/0.1uF pairs for a <a href="http://sim.okawa-denshi.jp/en/OPstool.php" target="_blank">fc=10Hz,Q=0.5</a><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sallen%E2%80%93Key_topology" target="_blank"> Sallen-Key</a> <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Low-pass" target="_blank">LPF</a> variation.</p><p><img src="https://cdn.hackaday.io/images/6255731407714701934.png"></p>
                                
                            </p>
                        </div>
