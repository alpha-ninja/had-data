                <div class="section section-description">
                    <h5>Description</h5>
                    <div class="description">My project uses a photocell and a microcontroller to monitor light pollution. My project aims to minimize light pollution and to track areas of excessive light pollution. My goal is to also build a website so that users will be able to log data from their light pollution monitor and view data from around the world taken by other users. In conclusion this project is meant to minimize light pollution and maximize our knowledge of light pollution. <br /><br />This project's source code is licensed under the MIT License. </div>
