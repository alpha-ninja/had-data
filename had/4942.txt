Place a small fountain pump  in the basin and control the run time of the pump with a simple outdoor timer . Place a hardware wire screen over the basin to keep leaves out. On the output side of the pump place a hose to ¼” adapter. Feed the flow from the pump into a micro irrigation kit. 

IMPORTANT NOTES: 
Do not connect anything directly to the condensate drip from the AC. It can clog and overflow the AC pan causing water damage in the house!
Be sure to place a screen over the basin to keep leaves from clogging the fountain pump.

                <div class="section section-description">
                    <h5>Description</h5>
                    <div class="description">Gather the water from the AC condensate drip into a basin. Place a small fountain pump  in the basin and control the run time of the pump with a simple outdoor timer . Place a hardware wire screen over the basin to keep leaves out. On the output side of the pump place a hose to ¼” adapter. Feed the flow from the pump into a micro irrigation kit. <br /><br />IMPORTANT NOTES: <br />Do not connect anything directly to the condensate drip from the AC. It can clog and overflow the AC pan causing water damage in the house!<br />Be sure to place a screen over the basin to keep leaves from clogging the fountain pump.<br />Add a mosquito larvicide to the basin.  The bacteria in the larvicide will kill the mosquitos without adding toxins to the irrigation system.<br /></div>
                </div>
