                <div id="comment-25521-content">
                    <p>You can't always think of something bad - you have to be able to walk down a dark road without fear and suspicion. With technology getting cheaper everyday - why don't &nbsp;we&nbsp;try&nbsp;to prevent shit from happening? There was a murder recently in the district where I'm from - an 18 y/o girl in a dark alley. You have to wonder how things could have been prevented. Sure, she could have listened to the streets and immediately call her parents&nbsp;for help / collecting her. But humans don't think of everything, especially in a stressful situation.&nbsp;</p><p>I don't see how this would replace intelligence, as this would probably only remind&nbsp;people to be careful. OTOH - it will&nbsp;be&nbsp;hard to tell the difference if it is actually okay that people are in your personal space. Sneaking up is one thing, but having people around in everyday&nbsp;situations is something you don't want to be alerted to.
