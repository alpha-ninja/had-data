Commercially available instruments for measuring personal exposure to diesel particulate matter cost many thousands of dollars and are, therefore, not practical for citizen science.<br /> <br />Our Diesel Soot Monitor uses the same operating principle as commercially available devices but has been designed as a tiny package and for ultra-low cost mass production (presently under $400 per instrument).  We are currently redesigning the circuitry and integrating flow and power control. The project is Arduino-based and uses open source code.<br /><br /></div>
