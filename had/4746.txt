                    <h5>Description</h5>
                    <div class="description">The core concept is a solar(or whatever renewable is appropriate) charging station for a swarm of robots. <br /><br />I&apos;d like this project to serve as a model for future projects in addition to the specific system i build this year.  <br />The only conditions to fit the model are: <br />-Two or more semi-autonomous robots that communicate with a separate central system.<br />-Renewable energy source if the robots need to charge(could be embedded in the robots themselves)<br />-Can&apos;t be used to kill anyone<br />-make up your own M.R.E.T.C.E.T.E.R.A. acronym to fit its porpoise. (have fun with it! It&apos;s mandatory)<br /></div>
                </div>
            

            
                <div class="section section-details">
                    <h5>
                        
                            Details
                        
                    </h5>
                    

                    
                        <div class="post-content details-content">
                            <p><p><br>This system will be geared towards environmental analysis and cleanup. Oftentimes there are places humans cannot or should not reach in any efforts to clean up pollution. An autonomous force that can be remote controlled if necessary could prove quite useful in these cases. </p><p>Ultimately the project will have many types of smaller robots who will all have the same charging apparatus but will differ in their functions. Some will collect details about the environment, while others alter it; Removing plastic or other pollutants from water for instance. Different pollutants will require different approaches and the goal is to have an &quot;ecosystem&quot; which will support multiple varieties of bots designed by anyone who wants to design and build one. <br></p><p>Initially the design will be comprised of two types of robots: A solar charging station and smaller scout robots. The scouts collect information about the surrounding environment and transmit it to a central database on the charging station which is then accessible to all the scout bots.<br></p>
                                
                            </p>
                        </div>
