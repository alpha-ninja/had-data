                    </h5>

                            <p><p>Design is simply velcroed flexible PCB that has our curved battery, 3dB BLE Flex Module, Qi/USB Charger Flex Module, and the LRA Haptic Flex Module and a 9mm Samsung LRA.  We might include the 12axis to provide a notice recognition gesture/movement.  Waiting on a solid Qi/USB Charger Flex Module.</p>
                                
                            </p>
