This project will use digital signal processing to isolate and invert the noise. A pair of microphones placed inside the ear cups will provide the feedback signal required in the project. 

The main objectives of this project are:

-Create a small board that can be placed inside the ear cup of large headphones, or in the headband of the smaller models.
-Be able to cancel at least 20 db at 1 kHz
-Have a decent battery life: several days on standby and at least 4 hours while playing at a reasonable volume
