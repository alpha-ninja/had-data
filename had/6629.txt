                <div class="section section-description">
                    <h5>Description</h5>
                    <div class="description">Government robotics companies build humanoid robots all the time.  These robots are extremely expensive and are not available to anyone in the hobbyist realm.  These robots rely on AI or autonomous code which is not or will ever be as reliable as the trained human mind.<br /><br />My team and I are creating a humanoid/tank robot.  This robot will be under 60 pounds, cost less than $2200 and can be made with off the shelf parts.  This robot is so cheap is can be made by ANYONE, even high school students.  It is made out of aluminum sheet metal and can be made in under a week.  The robot can even pull an additional 50 pounds of weight.<br /><br />The best part about the robot is that it mimics the upper human body movement in REAL TIME and all terrain.  Any user can control this robot from anywhere in the world and receive a real time video feed in a Virtual Reality manner.  This way we put the human mind 'inside' the robot, YET allowing the user to be completely safe from harm.<br />We call it: Shadow Bot</div>
                </div>

Claw</p><p><strong>Lower Body (Mechanical)</strong></p><p style="margin-left: 20px;">- Omni-Wheel Drive</p><p style="margin-left: 40px;">- Four 20 lb / in motors</p><p style="margin-left: 40px;">- 45 Degree Angle position</p><p style="margin-left: 60px;">- Allows for strafe</p><p style="margin-left: 60px;">- Diagonal movement</p><p><strong>Project Stages</strong><br></p><p style="margin-left: 20px;">- Stage 1</p><p style="margin-left: 40px;">- User wears a pair of gloves and stands in front of a camera</p><p style="margin-left: 40px;">- Current range is limited to range ~ 500 ft.</p><p style="margin-left: 40px;">- Glove data -&gt; Robot</p><p style="margin-left: 40px;">- Computer data -&gt; Black Box -&gt;  Robot</p>
